HTML, CSS, Javascript

HTML is the content of your page.
It is made up of all of the actual information you want to be displayed.

CSS is the styling of your page.
It makes the content (or HTML) look a certain way.
Most of the pages I make have relevant information on them, but don't look very pretty.

Javascript is the worker and mover of your page.
It is a client side language which can make your website more
dynamic by changing the HTML or CSS of your page without having to talk to the server.
